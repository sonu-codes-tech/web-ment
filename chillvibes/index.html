About Us

It all started in a tiny kitchen with one espresso machine and a dream. Founded in 2022, Chillvibes was born out of a love for community and a deep respect for the perfect brew. Today, we expand our cafe with some new food items and we remain dedicated to roasting locally-sourced beans and serving Jharsuguda with the same passion that sparked our journey.
